Hey, I'm Troy

I'm a front-end web developer and an avid player of table top RPG's like Dungeons and Dragons and Pathfinder. I created this site to tell the fun and wacky stories that inevitably come out of role playing games. You all the know the ones, Where your GM sets up for you to go to a tavern and you end up setting a world eating Tarrasque upon the planet. I aim for this to be a community project where people can send in their fantastic stories and mischief and magic for the world to read!
